about

Chris Hazzard & Michael Fontana are a comedy writing team, hailing from the East Coast and based in Los Angeles. They most recently adapted the novel Ex-Mas for Amazon Studios, penned the greenlight draft of Lionsgate’s musical comedy Under Cover starring Zachary Levi and Cole Sprouse, and their original feature comedy Adulting was just set up at Sony with Studio 8 producing.

They have also written feature projects for Mattel, MGM, Sony, Fox International, Warner Brothers, Netflix, and others.

On the TV side, they currently have an original comedy in active development at Hulu and a single-cam in the works at CBS. In addition to that, they wrote for Season 2 of the PopTV show Swedish Dicks starring Peter Stormare and Keanu Reeves and have also developed two original comedies for FX Networks.

Chris & Mike spend an almost nauseating amount of time together. Bordering on unhealthy and co-dependent, actually. 
But that’s neither here nor there.
